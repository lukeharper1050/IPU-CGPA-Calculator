How to Calculate CGPA in SRM University – Step-by-Step Process

  1. Collect your grade cards from all completed semesters.
  2. Note down the grade received in each subject (O, A+, A, B+, B, C, P, F, or Ab).
  3. Record the credits assigned to each subject (usually 3-4 for theory, 1-2 for lab).
  4. Convert each grade to grade points using the SRM grading table.
  5. Multiply grade points by credits for each subject to get “Grade Points Earned”.
  6. Add all grade points earned across all subjects in that semester.
  7. Divide by total credits attempted in that semester to find GPA.
  8. Repeat for all semesters and take the weighted average to get CGPA.

SRM CGPA Formula Explained

For Semester GPA:
GPA = (Σ Grade Points × Credits) ÷ (Σ Credits in that Semester)

For Cumulative CGPA:
CGPA = (Σ Grade Points × Credits across all Semesters) ÷ (Σ Total Credits of all Semesters)

Subjects with higher credits have more weight in your final CGPA, meaning a 4-credit subject impacts your CGPA more than a 1-credit lab.

SRM GPA vs CGPA – What’s the Difference?

GPA measures performance for a single semester, while CGPA reflects cumulative performance across all semesters.

Example:

  • Semester 1: 20 credits, 160 grade points → GPA = 8.00
  • Semester 2: 22 credits, 176 grade points → GPA = 8.00
  • Semester 3: 21 credits, 189 grade points → GPA = 9.00

CGPA = (160 + 176 + 189) ÷ (20 + 22 + 21) = 525 ÷ 63 = 8.33

GPA reflects short-term performance; CGPA represents long-term academic standing.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not including failed (F) or absent (Ab) subjects in CGPA.
  • Mixing up credits with grade points.
  • Using incorrect conversion formula (use only (CGPA - 0.5) × 10).
  • Calculating simple instead of weighted average.
  • Ignoring internship or seminar credits.
  • Using outdated grading scales (ensure correct regulation year).

FAQs – SRM CGPA Calculator (2025)

How does SRM calculate GPA and CGPA?
GPA = Σ (Grade Points × Credits) ÷ Σ Credits (per semester). CGPA = total grade points ÷ total credits (all semesters).

Does this calculator work for Regulation 2021?
Yes, it supports all regulations (2018, 2021, etc.) and uses the same 10-point scale.

How do I calculate my percentage from CGPA?
Use SRM’s official formula: (CGPA - 0.5) × 10.

What is a good CGPA at SRM University?
8.5+ is excellent, 7.5,8.5 is very good, 7.0,7.5 is good, and 6.0 is minimum to maintain academic standing.

Can I improve my CGPA later?
Yes, by performing better in future semesters focus on high-credit subjects for maximum impact.

What happens if I fail a subject?
It’s counted as 0 grade points until cleared. After passing, the new grade replaces the failed one in CGPA calculation.
